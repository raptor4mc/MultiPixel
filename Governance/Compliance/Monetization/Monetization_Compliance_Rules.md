# Monetization Compliance Rules
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Sections 5, 7, and 14 of the MultiPixel
Source-Available License 2.2 — Fortress Edition.

These rules define the mandatory requirements for all monetized activity
within the MultiPixel ecosystem. They apply to:

• Servers
• Mods
• Businesses
• Creators
• Integrations
• Any system generating revenue using MultiPixel technology, assets, or branding

These rules are enforceable. Violations may result in suspension,
delisting, financial penalties, or legal action.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — APPROVED MONETIZATION METHODS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following monetization methods are permitted ONLY if fully compliant
with this document and approved where required:

• Donations
• Subscriptions
• Cosmetic roles or cosmetic-only perks
• Cosmetic in-game items
• Server access passes (non-pay-to-win)
• Sponsorships
• Non-intrusive advertising
• Marketplace sales (mods, assets, content)
• Business partnerships approved by MultiPixel

The following are strictly prohibited:

• Pay-to-win mechanics
• Selling gameplay advantages
• Selling progression, stats, or power
• Lootboxes with gameplay impact
• Gambling or chance-based rewards
• Crypto, NFTs, or blockchain-based items
• Selling unapproved MultiPixel assets
• Selling unapproved derivative works
• Selling admin/moderator roles

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — REVENUE SHARE REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All monetized activity must comply with the mandatory revenue share:

• 30% for Servers
• 15% for Mods
• 10% for business integrations (unless otherwise negotiated)

Revenue share applies to:

• Donations
• Subscriptions
• Cosmetic sales
• Sponsorships
• Advertising revenue
• Marketplace sales
• Any monetized feature connected to MultiPixel

Revenue share does NOT apply to:

• External merchandise
• Off-platform brand deals
• Non-MultiPixel products

Entities must maintain accurate revenue records and submit reports as required.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — PAYMENT PLATFORM REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All monetized systems must use approved payment platforms:

• Stripe
• PayPal
• Patreon
• Ko-fi
• MultiPixel Marketplace (when available)

Servers and mods must NOT:

• Use unverified payment processors
• Accept cryptocurrency for gameplay-related purchases
• Use anonymous or untraceable payment systems

All payment logs must be retained for a minimum of 12 months.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4 — TRANSPARENCY & REPORTING
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All monetized entities must:

• Submit monthly revenue reports
• Provide accurate revenue totals
• Disclose all monetization methods
• Disclose all payment platforms used
• Provide audit access upon request

Failure to report revenue is treated as intentional concealment.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5 — AUDIT REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

MultiPixel reserves the right to audit any monetized entity.

Audits may include:

• Revenue verification
• Payment platform review
• Server or mod inspection
• Code review (for monetization logic)
• Log verification
• User impact analysis

Entities must:

• Cooperate fully
• Provide requested documents within 7 days
• Not delete or alter logs
• Not obstruct the audit process

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6 — USER PROTECTION RULES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All monetized systems must:

• Clearly disclose prices
• Clearly disclose what users receive
• Avoid deceptive or manipulative design
• Provide refunds where required by law
• Avoid pressure-based monetization

Servers and mods must NOT:

• Target minors with monetization
• Use dark patterns
• Hide gameplay behind paywalls
• Misrepresent cosmetic items

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7 — BRANDING & LICENSE COMPLIANCE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Monetized entities must comply with:

• Branding Compliance Rules
• MultiPixel License 2.2
• Server Compliance Requirements
• Mod Compliance Requirements

Entities must NOT:

• Imply official partnership without approval
• Use MultiPixel branding in paid items without permission
• Sell MultiPixel assets or derivatives without authorization

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8 — VIOLATION SEVERITY LEVELS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Minor Violations:
• Incorrect reporting format
• Delayed revenue submission
• Cosmetic mislabeling

Moderate Violations:
• Missing revenue reports
• Unapproved monetization methods
• Failure to disclose sponsorships

Severe Violations:
• Pay-to-win systems
• Revenue concealment
• Unauthorized asset sales
• Refusal to cooperate with audits
• Deceptive monetization practices

Severe violations may result in immediate suspension or legal action.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 9 — ENFORCEMENT
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

MultiPixel may enforce these rules through:

• Warnings
• Temporary suspension
• Permanent suspension
• Removal from listings
• Financial penalties
• Legal enforcement

All enforcement actions are final.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 10 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

These rules may be updated at any time.
All monetized entities must remain compliant with the latest version.
