# Prohibited Monetization Methods
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Section 1 of the Monetization Compliance Rules and
Sections 5, 7, and 14 of the MultiPixel Source-Available License 2.2 —
Fortress Edition.

It defines all monetization methods that are strictly forbidden within the
MultiPixel ecosystem. These prohibitions apply to:

• Servers
• Mods
• Businesses
• Creators
• Integrations
• Any system generating revenue using MultiPixel technology, assets, or branding

If a monetization method is not explicitly listed as allowed, it is
prohibited by default.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — PAY-TO-WIN SYSTEMS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are strictly prohibited:

• Selling gameplay advantages  
• Selling stat boosts  
• Selling damage, health, armor, or defense  
• Selling XP multipliers  
• Selling resource multipliers  
• Selling progression shortcuts  
• Selling exclusive gameplay zones  
• Selling exclusive loot or drops  
• Selling items that alter game balance  

Any system where money affects gameplay outcomes is forbidden.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — PROGRESSION & POWER SALES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following may NOT be sold under any circumstances:

• Levels  
• Skills  
• Abilities  
• Unlocks  
• Crafting recipes  
• Weapons  
• Tools  
• Armor  
• Vehicles  
• Gameplay-affecting pets or mounts  
• Any item that increases player power  

Progression must be earned through gameplay only.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — GAMBLING & CHANCE-BASED SYSTEMS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are strictly prohibited:

• Lootboxes with gameplay impact  
• Chance-based item rolls  
• Randomized stat items  
• Gacha systems  
• Slot-machine mechanics  
• Wheel spins with gameplay rewards  
• Paid RNG-based crafting  
• Paid mystery boxes  

Cosmetic-only lootboxes may be allowed **only with explicit approval**.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — CRYPTO, NFTS, AND BLOCKCHAIN
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are absolutely forbidden:

• Cryptocurrency payments for gameplay  
• NFT items  
• Blockchain-based assets  
• Tokenized items  
• Web3 integrations  
• Play-to-earn systems  
• Crypto-based progression  
• Blockchain-based identity systems  

No exceptions.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — UNAPPROVED ASSET SALES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following may NOT be sold:

• MultiPixel assets  
• Modified MultiPixel assets  
• Derivative works without approval  
• AI-generated recreations of MultiPixel assets  
• Repackaged or reskinned MultiPixel content  
• Any asset that resembles official MultiPixel branding  

All assets must be original or approved.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — UNAPPROVED MOD MONETIZATION
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are prohibited:

• Selling Mods without approval  
• Selling Mod features  
• Selling Mod access  
• Selling Mod updates  
• Selling Mod-exclusive gameplay advantages  
• Selling Mod-based progression  

All Mods are derivative works and require approval before monetization.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — UNAPPROVED SERVER MONETIZATION
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers may NOT:

• Monetize without approval (if required)  
• Sell gameplay advantages  
• Sell admin or moderator roles  
• Sell staff permissions  
• Sell ban immunity  
• Sell whitelist access that affects gameplay fairness  
• Sell access to exclusive gameplay content  

Servers must follow the Server Compliance Requirements.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — DECEPTIVE OR MANIPULATIVE MONETIZATION
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are prohibited:

• Dark patterns  
• Hidden fees  
• Misleading pricing  
• Fake discounts  
• Time-limited pressure sales  
• “Pay now or lose progress” systems  
• Monetization targeting minors  
• Monetization disguised as gameplay  

All monetization must be transparent and honest.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — ADVERTISING VIOLATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are prohibited:

• Intrusive ads  
• Ads that interrupt gameplay  
• Forced video ads  
• Ads that block UI or controls  
• Ads promoting harmful or illegal content  
• Ads impersonating MultiPixel branding  
• Ads targeting minors with monetization  

Advertising must be non-intrusive and safe.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 10 — SPONSORSHIP VIOLATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are prohibited:

• Undisclosed sponsorships  
• Political sponsorships (unless approved)  
• Sponsorships that alter gameplay  
• Sponsorships that mislead users  
• Sponsorships implying official partnership  

All sponsorships must be transparent and compliant.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 11 — PAYMENT PLATFORM VIOLATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are prohibited:

• Unverified payment processors  
• Anonymous or untraceable payment systems  
• Cryptocurrency payments for gameplay  
• Payment systems that hide revenue  
• Payment systems that bypass reporting  

All payments must use approved platforms.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 12 — REVENUE CONCEALMENT
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are severe violations:

• Hiding revenue  
• Underreporting revenue  
• Using alternate accounts to bypass reporting  
• Using off-platform payments to avoid revenue share  
• Deleting payment logs  
• Falsifying financial records  

Revenue concealment triggers immediate enforcement.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 13 — GENERAL PROHIBITIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following are prohibited across all monetized systems:

• Monetization that affects fairness  
• Monetization that affects safety  
• Monetization that affects gameplay balance  
• Monetization that violates branding rules  
• Monetization that violates data rules  
• Monetization that violates server or mod rules  

If a monetization method is not explicitly allowed, it is prohibited.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 14 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document may be updated at any time.  
All monetized entities must comply with the latest version.
