# Allowed Monetization Methods
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Section 1 of the Monetization Compliance Rules and
Sections 5, 7, and 14 of the MultiPixel Source-Available License 2.2 —
Fortress Edition.

It defines all monetization methods that are permitted within the MultiPixel
ecosystem, provided they comply with:

• Monetization_Compliance_Rules  
• Revenue_Reporting_Requirements  
• Monetization_Audit_Procedure  
• Branding_Compliance_Rules  
• Server_Compliance_Requirements  
• Mod_Compliance_Requirements  

No monetization method is allowed unless explicitly listed here.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — DONATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Donations are allowed under the following conditions:

• They must be voluntary.  
• They must not grant gameplay advantages.  
• They must not unlock progression, stats, or power.  
• They must not be tied to gambling or chance-based rewards.  
• They must be processed through approved payment platforms.  

Allowed donation incentives:

• cosmetic badges  
• cosmetic titles  
• cosmetic-only supporter icons  
• public thank-you messages  

Not allowed:

• “donation ranks” with gameplay perks  
• “pay-to-skip” systems  
• donation-based lootboxes  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — SUBSCRIPTIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Subscriptions are allowed if:

• they provide cosmetic or quality-of-life benefits only  
• they do not provide gameplay advantages  
• they do not accelerate progression  
• they do not modify game balance  
• they are clearly labeled and priced  
• they use approved payment platforms  

Allowed subscription perks:

• cosmetic titles  
• cosmetic trails, particles, or effects  
• cosmetic-only VIP areas  
• cosmetic pets  
• cosmetic UI themes  

Not allowed:

• XP boosts  
• stat boosts  
• resource multipliers  
• priority gameplay advantages  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — COSMETIC ITEMS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Cosmetic items are allowed if:

• they do not affect gameplay  
• they do not modify stats, damage, health, or progression  
• they do not provide competitive advantages  
• they are clearly labeled as cosmetic  

Allowed cosmetic categories:

• skins  
• outfits  
• emotes  
• animations  
• particle effects  
• trails  
• banners  
• cosmetic pets  
• cosmetic mounts  
• cosmetic UI themes  

Not allowed:

• cosmetic items that secretly alter hitboxes  
• cosmetic items that obscure visibility  
• cosmetic items that mimic official MultiPixel branding without approval  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — SERVER ACCESS PASSES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers may charge for access if:

• the Server is approved (if required)  
• the pass grants access only, not gameplay advantages  
• the pass does not unlock exclusive progression  
• the pass does not create a pay-to-win environment  

Allowed:

• one-time access fee  
• monthly access subscription  
• cosmetic-only VIP areas  

Not allowed:

• “premium worlds” with better loot  
• “paid-only” gameplay systems  
• “exclusive progression zones”  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — SPONSORSHIPS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sponsorships are allowed if:

• they are disclosed  
• they do not influence gameplay  
• they do not promote harmful or illegal content  
• they do not misrepresent official MultiPixel partnership  
• they comply with branding rules  

Allowed sponsorship types:

• creator sponsorships  
• server sponsorships  
• mod sponsorships  
• brand placements (non-intrusive)  

Not allowed:

• political sponsorships (unless approved)  
• sponsorships that alter gameplay  
• sponsorships that mislead users  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — ADVERTISING
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Advertising is allowed if:

• it is non-intrusive  
• it does not interrupt gameplay  
• it does not mislead users  
• it does not promote harmful content  
• it does not impersonate official MultiPixel branding  

Allowed:

• banner ads in menus  
• sponsor panels  
• loading screen ads  
• cosmetic-only ad integrations  

Not allowed:

• mid-game popups  
• forced video ads  
• ads that block gameplay  
• ads that target minors with monetization  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — MARKETPLACE SALES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Creators may sell:

• mods (if approved)  
• cosmetic assets  
• cosmetic items  
• cosmetic packs  
• sound packs  
• UI themes  
• non-gameplay content  

All marketplace sales must:

• be approved (if required)  
• comply with revenue share rules  
• use approved payment platforms  
• follow branding and asset protection rules  

Not allowed:

• selling MultiPixel assets  
• selling derivative works without approval  
• selling gameplay advantages  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — BUSINESS INTEGRATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Businesses may monetize integrations if:

• they are approved  
• they comply with revenue share rules  
• they do not alter gameplay balance  
• they do not introduce gambling or crypto  
• they do not violate branding rules  

Allowed:

• cosmetic brand partnerships  
• cosmetic-only integrations  
• creator collaborations  
• non-gameplay business features  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — GENERAL CONDITIONS FOR ALL ALLOWED METHODS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All allowed monetization must:

• be transparent  
• be clearly labeled  
• be non-deceptive  
• avoid dark patterns  
• avoid targeting minors  
• avoid gameplay impact  
• comply with all reporting requirements  
• comply with all audit requirements  
• comply with all branding rules  
• comply with all server/mod compliance rules  

If a monetization method is not listed here, it is prohibited by default.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 10 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document may be updated at any time.  
All monetized entities must comply with the latest version.
