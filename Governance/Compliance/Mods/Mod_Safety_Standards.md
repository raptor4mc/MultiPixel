# Mod Safety Standards
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Sections 5, 7, 10, 12, and 14 of the MultiPixel
Source-Available License 2.2 — Fortress Edition.

It defines the mandatory safety requirements for all Mods within the
MultiPixel ecosystem. These standards exist to protect users, maintain
ecosystem integrity, and prevent harmful or abusive content.

ALL Mods must comply with these standards at all times.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — GENERAL SAFETY PRINCIPLES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All Mods must operate according to the following principles:

• user safety  
• fairness  
• transparency  
• non-harmful gameplay  
• no exploitation  
• no harassment  
• no abusive mechanics  
• no unsafe content  

Mods must NOT:

• endanger users  
• enable predatory behavior  
• introduce harmful mechanics  
• encourage harassment or abuse  
• include illegal or extremist content  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — PROHIBITED CONTENT
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following content is strictly prohibited in all Mods:

### 2.1 Illegal or Harmful Content
• sexual content involving minors  
• extremist content  
• hate speech  
• targeted harassment  
• doxxing or personal information  
• threats or violence against real individuals  

### 2.2 Unsafe Gameplay Mechanics
• mechanics that encourage self-harm  
• mechanics that simulate abuse  
• mechanics that enable predatory behavior  
• mechanics that trap or isolate players without consent  

### 2.3 Psychological Manipulation
• fear-based coercion  
• forced participation in disturbing content  
• deceptive or manipulative UI  
• dark patterns  

### 2.4 Real-World Political Extremism
• extremist symbols  
• extremist ideologies  
• extremist propaganda  

See: Mod_Content_Restrictions.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — ALLOWED BUT RESTRICTED CONTENT
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Some content is allowed but requires **full review** and may require
additional warnings or restrictions.

### 3.1 Mature Themes (Non-Explicit)
• mild horror  
• mild violence  
• mild dark fantasy themes  

### 3.2 Political Content (Non-Extremist)
• fictional political systems  
• educational political content  
• non-partisan real-world political references  

### 3.3 Competitive Gameplay
• PvP systems  
• combat mechanics  
• competitive challenges  

These must NOT:

• encourage harassment  
• create unfair advantages  
• enable bullying or abuse  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — SAFETY MECHANICS REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must include safety mechanisms appropriate to their content.

### 4.1 Content Warnings
Mods must provide warnings for:

• mature themes  
• horror content  
• flashing lights  
• loud or sudden audio  
• intense combat  
• political content  

### 4.2 Opt-In Systems
Mods with sensitive content must:

• require explicit opt-in  
• allow players to disable sensitive features  
• provide safe alternatives  

### 4.3 Parental Controls
Mods must respect:

• parental control settings  
• age restrictions  
• content filters  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — MULTIPLAYER SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods that interact with servers or multiplayer environments must:

• prevent harassment  
• prevent abuse  
• prevent griefing  
• prevent exploitation  
• prevent unfair advantages  

Mods must NOT:

• bypass server moderation  
• disable safety filters  
• enable unlogged actions  
• enable unmoderated communication channels  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — DATA SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• avoid collecting personal data  
• avoid collecting sensitive data  
• disclose all telemetry  
• use approved data systems  
• follow retention and deletion rules  

Mods must NOT:

• track users across servers  
• fingerprint users  
• store data insecurely  
• transmit data without approval  

See: Mod_Data_Requirements.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — TECHNICAL SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• avoid crashes  
• avoid data corruption  
• avoid unsafe memory usage  
• avoid infinite loops  
• avoid excessive CPU/GPU usage  
• avoid unsafe threading  
• avoid unauthorized file access  

Mods must NOT:

• modify engine binaries  
• bypass sandboxing  
• inject malicious code  
• weaken security systems  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — COMMUNITY SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must NOT:

• encourage harassment  
• encourage bullying  
• encourage discrimination  
• encourage exploitation  
• encourage unsafe behavior  

Mods must:

• promote fair play  
• promote respectful interaction  
• avoid harmful social mechanics  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — SAFETY AUDIT REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must provide:

• safety documentation  
• content warnings  
• code for review  
• data collection details  
• gameplay descriptions  
• risk assessments (if applicable)  

Refusal to provide safety information is a severe violation.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 10 — ENFORCEMENT
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Violations of these standards may result in:

• removal from the Mod Marketplace  
• suspension of approval  
• permanent bans  
• legal action  
• license termination  

See:
• Mod_Violation_Categories.md  
• Mod_Penalty_Tiers.md  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 11 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document may be updated at any time.  
All Mods must comply with the latest version.
