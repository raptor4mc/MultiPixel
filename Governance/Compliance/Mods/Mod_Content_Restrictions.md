# Mod Content Restrictions
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Sections 5, 7, 10, 12, and 14 of the MultiPixel
Source-Available License 2.2 — Fortress Edition.

It defines the allowed, restricted, and prohibited content for all Mods
within the MultiPixel ecosystem.

These rules exist to protect users, maintain ecosystem integrity, and
prevent harmful, illegal, or abusive content.

ALL Mods must comply with these restrictions at all times.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — GENERAL CONTENT PRINCIPLES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All Mods must follow these principles:

• user safety  
• fairness  
• non-harmful content  
• no illegal content  
• no extremist content  
• no hateful content  
• no sexual content involving minors  
• no abusive mechanics  
• no deceptive content  

Mods must NOT:

• endanger users  
• encourage harassment  
• promote discrimination  
• include illegal or extremist material  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — PROHIBITED CONTENT (ABSOLUTE BAN)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following content is strictly prohibited in all Mods.  
Including any of these results in an **automatic severe violation**.

### 2.1 Illegal or Harmful Content
• sexual content involving minors  
• child exploitation  
• real-world illegal activity  
• doxxing or personal information  
• threats or targeted violence  

### 2.2 Extremist or Hateful Content
• extremist symbols  
• extremist ideologies  
• extremist propaganda  
• hate speech  
• discriminatory content targeting protected groups  

### 2.3 Abusive or Predatory Mechanics
• harassment systems  
• stalking or tracking mechanics  
• forced interaction systems  
• mechanics that trap players without consent  
• mechanics that simulate abuse  

### 2.4 Unsafe Psychological Content
• content encouraging self-harm  
• content encouraging dangerous behavior  
• manipulative or coercive mechanics  
• dark patterns  

### 2.5 Prohibited Gameplay Content
• pay-to-win mechanics  
• gambling systems  
• crypto/NFT systems  
• malicious traps disguised as normal content  

### 2.6 Malicious or Deceptive Content
• malware  
• backdoors  
• unauthorized telemetry  
• hidden data collection  
• code obfuscation used to hide harmful behavior  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — RESTRICTED CONTENT (REQUIRES FULL REVIEW)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following content is allowed **only with approval** and may require
warnings, age restrictions, or additional safety measures.

### 3.1 Mature Themes (Non-Explicit)
• mild horror  
• mild violence  
• dark fantasy themes  
• non-graphic combat  

### 3.2 Political Content (Non-Extremist)
• fictional political systems  
• educational political content  
• non-partisan real-world references  

Political content must NOT:

• promote real-world extremist ideologies  
• encourage harassment  
• mislead users about real-world events  

### 3.3 Intense Gameplay Content
• high-difficulty combat  
• survival mechanics  
• intense audio/visual effects  
• jump scares  

These require:

• content warnings  
• opt-in systems  
• accessibility options  

### 3.4 Competitive Systems
• PvP mechanics  
• ranked systems  
• competitive challenges  

These must NOT:

• encourage bullying  
• create unfair advantages  
• enable harassment  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — ALLOWED CONTENT (STANDARD REVIEW)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following content is generally allowed:

### 4.1 Cosmetic Content
• textures  
• models  
• animations  
• sound packs  
• UI skins  

### 4.2 Gameplay Additions
• new items  
• new blocks  
• new mobs  
• new biomes  
• new structures  
• new worldgen features  

### 4.3 Quality-of-Life Features
• inventory improvements  
• UI enhancements  
• accessibility features  

### 4.4 Creative Content
• story content  
• adventure content  
• puzzle content  
• building tools  

Allowed content must still follow:

• safety rules  
• data rules  
• monetization rules  
• approval rules  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — CONTENT WARNING REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must provide clear warnings for:

• horror content  
• flashing lights  
• loud or sudden audio  
• mature themes  
• political content  
• intense combat  

Warnings must be:

• visible  
• accurate  
• honest  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — PLAYER CONSENT REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods with sensitive or intense content must:

• require explicit opt-in  
• allow disabling sensitive features  
• provide safe alternatives  

Players must NEVER be forced into:

• disturbing content  
• political content  
• intense horror  
• unsafe mechanics  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — MULTIPLAYER CONTENT RULES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods used in multiplayer must NOT:

• enable harassment  
• enable griefing  
• bypass moderation  
• disable safety filters  
• enable unlogged actions  
• create unfair advantages  

Multiplayer mods must:

• respect server rules  
• respect moderation systems  
• maintain fairness  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — ENFORCEMENT
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Violations of these restrictions may result in:

• removal from the Mod Marketplace  
• suspension of approval  
• permanent bans  
• legal action  
• license termination  

See:
• Mod_Violation_Categories.md  
• Mod_Penalty_Tiers.md  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document may be updated at any time.  
All Mods must comply with the latest version.
