# Mod Penalty Tiers
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Sections 7, 10, 12, and 14 of the MultiPixel
Source-Available License 2.2 — Fortress Edition.

It defines the official penalty tiers for Mod violations within the
MultiPixel ecosystem. Penalties are based on the severity of the violation
and the developer’s compliance history.

Penalty tiers are divided into:
• Tier 1 — Minor Penalties  
• Tier 2 — Major Penalties  
• Tier 3 — Severe Penalties  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — TIER 1: MINOR PENALTIES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tier 1 penalties apply to Minor Violations that do not threaten user
safety, platform security, or ecosystem integrity.

### 1.1 Applicable Violations
• incorrect metadata  
• missing tags  
• inaccurate descriptions  
• minor compatibility issues  
• missing content warnings  
• incomplete documentation  
• minor UI bugs  
• non-harmful scripting errors  

### 1.2 Penalties
• warning notice  
• required fix within a set timeframe  
• temporary listing pause (if needed)  
• required resubmission after fixes  

### 1.3 Escalation Conditions
Minor penalties escalate if:

• the issue is not fixed  
• the issue repeats  
• the developer ignores communication  
• multiple Minor Violations accumulate  

Escalation moves the violation to Tier 2.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — TIER 2: MAJOR PENALTIES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tier 2 penalties apply to Major Violations that impact fairness,
transparency, stability, or compliance but do not involve malicious intent.

### 2.1 Applicable Violations
• hidden monetization  
• unapproved monetization  
• unapproved data collection  
• misleading descriptions  
• significant performance degradation  
• unbalanced gameplay systems  
• unauthorized external communication  
• failure to disclose telemetry  
• distributing updates without approval  

### 2.2 Penalties
• immediate suspension of the Mod  
• removal from the Mod Marketplace (temporary)  
• forced rollback to previous approved version  
• monetization freeze  
• required full review of updated version  
• mandatory audit  
• probation period for future updates  

### 2.3 Escalation Conditions
Major penalties escalate if:

• the issue is repeated  
• the developer attempts to hide violations  
• the violation causes user harm  
• the violation impacts ecosystem integrity  

Escalation moves the violation to Tier 3.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — TIER 3: SEVERE PENALTIES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tier 3 penalties apply to Severe Violations that threaten user safety,
platform security, legal compliance, or the integrity of the entire
ecosystem.

### 3.1 Applicable Violations
• malware  
• backdoors  
• unauthorized telemetry  
• data harvesting  
• personal data collection  
• extremist content  
• hate speech  
• sexual content involving minors  
• gambling systems  
• crypto/NFT monetization  
• pay-to-win systems  
• bypassing engine security  
• code obfuscation used to hide harmful behavior  
• impersonating official MultiPixel content  
• distributing Mods without approval  
• bypassing the approval system  
• bypassing audits  
• exploiting vulnerabilities intentionally  

### 3.2 Penalties
• immediate removal of the Mod  
• permanent ban of the Mod  
• permanent developer ban (if applicable)  
• permanent monetization ban  
• revocation of all approvals  
• full ecosystem audit of all developer Mods  
• legal action (if applicable)  
• license termination  

### 3.3 Zero Tolerance Policy
Severe Violations are treated with zero tolerance.  
No warnings. No grace periods. No exceptions.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — DEVELOPER ACCOUNT PENALTIES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Penalties may apply to the developer account, not just the Mod.

### 4.1 Account-Level Actions
• probation  
• temporary suspension  
• permanent suspension  
• permanent ban  
• removal of all Mods  
• revocation of monetization rights  

### 4.2 Conditions for Account Penalties
Account penalties apply when:

• multiple Mods violate rules  
• the developer repeatedly violates rules  
• the developer acts maliciously  
• the developer attempts to bypass enforcement  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — MONETIZATION PENALTIES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Monetization penalties apply when violations involve revenue.

### 5.1 Possible Actions
• monetization freeze  
• monetization suspension  
• permanent monetization ban  
• revenue clawback (if fraud occurred)  
• removal from the marketplace  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — AUDIT-RELATED PENALTIES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

If a developer refuses to cooperate with audits:

• immediate suspension of all Mods  
• forced removal from the marketplace  
• permanent ban (if refusal continues)  
• legal action (if required by the License)  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — ESCALATION FRAMEWORK
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Violations escalate based on:

• severity  
• repetition  
• intent  
• user harm  
• ecosystem impact  
• developer cooperation  

Escalation is automatic when conditions are met.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — APPEALS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Developers may submit an appeal for:

• Tier 1 penalties  
• Tier 2 penalties  

Tier 3 penalties are **not** eligible for appeal except in cases of proven error.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document may be updated at any time.  
All Mods must comply with the latest version.
