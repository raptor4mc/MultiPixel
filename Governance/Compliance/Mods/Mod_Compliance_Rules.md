# Mod Compliance Rules
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Sections 5, 7, 10, 12, and 14 of the MultiPixel
Source-Available License 2.2 — Fortress Edition.

It defines the global rules that ALL Mods must follow within the MultiPixel
ecosystem. These rules apply to every Mod, regardless of size, purpose,
complexity, or distribution method.

ALL Mods must be approved before distribution.  
ALL Mods must comply with these rules at all times.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — GENERAL COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All Mods must:

• comply with the MultiPixel License  
• comply with all Mod Governance documents  
• comply with all safety standards  
• comply with all data and telemetry rules  
• comply with all monetization rules  
• comply with all content restrictions  
• comply with all audit requirements  
• maintain compatibility with the MultiPixel engine  
• maintain stability and security  

Mods must NOT:

• violate the License  
• introduce unsafe or harmful content  
• introduce malicious or deceptive behavior  
• bypass engine security  
• impersonate official MultiPixel content  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — APPROVAL REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

ALL Mods require approval before they may be:

• uploaded  
• shared  
• distributed  
• published  
• monetized  
• used on public servers  

No Mod may bypass the approval system.

See: Mod_Approval_Requirements.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• use supported APIs  
• use stable engine features  
• avoid deprecated functions  
• maintain performance standards  
• avoid excessive resource usage  
• avoid memory leaks  
• avoid infinite loops  
• avoid unsafe threading  
• avoid unauthorized file access  

Mods must NOT:

• modify engine binaries  
• modify protected systems  
• bypass sandboxing  
• inject code into the client  
• alter network protocols  
• introduce exploits  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• be free of malware  
• be free of backdoors  
• be free of unauthorized telemetry  
• be free of obfuscated malicious code  
• use secure coding practices  
• protect user safety  

Mods must NOT:

• collect personal data  
• transmit data without disclosure  
• access external servers without approval  
• modify security settings  
• weaken engine protections  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — CONTENT REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• follow content restrictions  
• avoid harmful or illegal content  
• avoid extremist or hateful content  
• avoid sexual content involving minors  
• avoid unsafe gameplay mechanics  
• avoid deceptive or misleading content  

See: Mod_Content_Restrictions.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — MONETIZATION REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• follow monetization rules  
• disclose all monetization methods  
• use approved payment platforms  
• avoid pay-to-win systems  
• avoid gambling systems  
• avoid crypto/NFT monetization  

See: Mod_Monetization_Rules.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — DATA & TELEMETRY REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• disclose all data collection  
• use approved telemetry methods  
• follow retention and deletion rules  
• avoid collecting personal or sensitive data  

See: Mod_Data_Requirements.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• avoid harmful gameplay  
• avoid unsafe mechanics  
• avoid harassment or abuse systems  
• avoid bypassing safety filters  
• avoid enabling predatory behavior  

See: Mod_Safety_Standards.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — OPERATIONAL REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• maintain stability  
• maintain compatibility  
• maintain documentation  
• maintain update logs  
• maintain versioning  
• maintain support for major engine updates  

Mods must NOT:

• break core gameplay  
• cause crashes  
• cause data corruption  
• degrade performance intentionally  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 10 — DISTRIBUTION REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mods must:

• be distributed only through approved channels  
• include accurate descriptions  
• include accurate tags  
• include accurate warnings  
• include accurate version information  

Mods must NOT:

• misrepresent features  
• hide monetization  
• hide data collection  
• impersonate official content  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 11 — ENFORCEMENT
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Violations of these rules may result in:

• removal from the Mod Marketplace  
• suspension of approval  
• monetization bans  
• delisting  
• legal action  
• license termination  

See:
• Mod_Violation_Categories.md  
• Mod_Penalty_Tiers.md  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 12 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document may be updated at any time.  
All Mods must comply with the latest version.
