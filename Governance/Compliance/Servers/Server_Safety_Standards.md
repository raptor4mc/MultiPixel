# Server Safety Standards
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Sections 6, 8, 10, 12, and 15 of the MultiPixel
Source-Available License 2.2 — Fortress Edition.

It defines the mandatory safety requirements for all Servers operating
within the MultiPixel ecosystem.

These standards apply to:
• Public Servers
• Private Servers
• Community Servers
• Business Servers
• Political Servers
• Monetized Servers

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — SAFETY PRINCIPLES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All Servers must operate according to the following principles:

• protect users from harm  
• prevent harassment and abuse  
• prevent hate speech and extremist content  
• protect minors  
• maintain a safe and respectful environment  
• enforce rules consistently  
• respond to safety incidents promptly  

Servers must NOT:

• encourage unsafe behavior  
• allow targeted harassment  
• allow hate speech  
• allow extremist content  
• allow sexual content involving minors  
• allow dangerous or harmful activities  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — REQUIRED SAFETY SYSTEMS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must implement:

### 2.1 Moderation Tools
• ban system  
• mute system  
• kick system  
• warning system  
• staff action logging  

### 2.2 Safety Automation
• chat filters  
• spam detection  
• anti-harassment filters  
• anti-extremism filters  
• anti-grooming protections (if minors are present)  

### 2.3 Reporting Tools
• in-game reporting  
• out-of-game reporting  
• staff escalation channels  

### 2.4 Staff Requirements
• trained moderators  
• clear staff roles  
• staff accountability  
• staff behavior guidelines  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — PROHIBITED CONTENT
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must NOT allow:

### 3.1 Hate Speech
• slurs  
• dehumanizing language  
• attacks on protected groups  
• extremist ideology  

### 3.2 Harassment & Abuse
• targeted harassment  
• bullying  
• stalking  
• threats  
• doxxing  

### 3.3 Sexual Content
• sexual content involving minors  
• grooming behavior  
• sexual harassment  
• explicit sexual content  

### 3.4 Violence & Harm
• threats of violence  
• encouragement of self-harm  
• glorification of real-world violence  
• dangerous challenges or activities  

### 3.5 Extremism
• extremist symbols  
• extremist propaganda  
• recruitment or radicalization  

### 3.6 Illegal Content
• illegal media  
• illegal distribution  
• illegal activities  

Any violation of this section is a **severe violation**.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — MINOR SAFETY
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers with minors must:

• enforce age-appropriate content  
• prevent adult content  
• prevent grooming behavior  
• prevent predatory behavior  
• restrict voice chat if necessary  
• restrict private messaging if necessary  

Servers must NOT:

• allow adults to privately message minors  
• allow minors to access unsafe content  
• allow minors to participate in monetization targeting them  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — SAFETY INCIDENT RESPONSE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must:

### 5.1 Respond Quickly
• acknowledge reports  
• investigate promptly  
• take action within reasonable time  

### 5.2 Document Actions
• log all moderation actions  
• log all safety incidents  
• retain logs for required periods  

### 5.3 Escalate When Necessary
• escalate severe incidents to MultiPixel  
• escalate illegal incidents to authorities (if required by law)  

### 5.4 Protect Users
• remove harmful users  
• restrict dangerous behavior  
• prevent retaliation  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — STAFF BEHAVIOR REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Server staff must:

• act professionally  
• avoid favoritism  
• avoid abuse of power  
• avoid harassment  
• avoid conflicts of interest  

Staff must NOT:

• harass users  
• retaliate against reports  
• use moderation tools for personal gain  
• sell moderation or admin roles  
• ignore safety incidents  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — SAFETY LOGGING REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must maintain logs for:

• bans  
• warnings  
• mutes  
• kicks  
• staff actions  
• safety incidents  
• rule enforcement  

Logs must be:

• accurate  
• timestamped  
• unmodified  
• securely stored  

Deleting safety logs is a **severe violation**.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — SAFETY VIOLATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Examples of **minor violations**:
• missing safety documentation  
• outdated safety disclosures  

Examples of **moderate violations**:
• incomplete moderation logs  
• slow response to safety incidents  
• insufficient chat filtering  

Examples of **severe violations**:
• allowing hate speech  
• allowing harassment  
• allowing extremist content  
• allowing sexual content involving minors  
• ignoring safety reports  
• staff abusing power  
• deleting safety logs  

See:
• Server_Violation_Categories.md  
• Server_Penalty_Tiers.md  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — AUDIT REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must provide:

• safety logs  
• moderation logs  
• staff lists  
• safety system configuration  
• chat filter configuration  
• incident reports  

Refusal to provide safety data during an audit is a **severe violation**.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 10 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document may be updated at any time.  
All Servers must comply with the latest version.
