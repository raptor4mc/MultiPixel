# Server Monetization Rules
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Sections 5, 7, 12, and 14 of the MultiPixel
Source-Available License 2.2 — Fortress Edition, and integrates with the
Monetization Compliance Suite.

It defines the monetization rules specific to Servers operating within the
MultiPixel ecosystem.

These rules apply to:
• Public Servers
• Private Servers
• Community Servers
• Business Servers
• Political Servers
• Any Server generating revenue using MultiPixel technology

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — APPROVAL REQUIREMENTS FOR MONETIZED SERVERS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

A Server MUST obtain approval before monetizing if it:

• accepts donations  
• sells subscriptions  
• sells cosmetic items  
• sells access passes  
• uses sponsorships  
• uses advertising  
• sells marketplace content  
• receives barter or non-cash value  
• uses any monetization method listed in the Monetization Compliance Rules  

Operating a monetized Server without approval is a **severe violation**.

See: Server_Approval_Requirements.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — ALLOWED SERVER MONETIZATION METHODS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers may ONLY use monetization methods listed in:

• Allowed_Monetization_Methods.md  

Allowed examples include:

• voluntary donations  
• cosmetic-only subscriptions  
• cosmetic item sales  
• cosmetic VIP areas  
• non-intrusive advertising  
• sponsorships (with disclosure)  
• access passes (non-pay-to-win)  

Servers must clearly label all monetized features.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — PROHIBITED SERVER MONETIZATION METHODS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must NOT use any monetization method listed in:

• Prohibited_Monetization_Methods.md  

Prohibited examples include:

• pay-to-win systems  
• selling progression, stats, or power  
• selling weapons, armor, or gameplay items  
• selling admin/mod roles  
• gambling or chance-based rewards  
• crypto/NFT monetization  
• lootboxes with gameplay impact  
• revenue concealment  
• unapproved payment processors  

Any prohibited monetization method is a **severe violation**.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — PAYMENT PLATFORM REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must use ONLY approved payment platforms:

• Stripe  
• PayPal  
• Patreon  
• Ko-fi  
• MultiPixel Marketplace (when available)  

Servers must NOT:

• use unverified payment processors  
• use anonymous or untraceable payment systems  
• accept cryptocurrency for gameplay-related purchases  

Servers must retain payment logs for at least 12 months.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — REVENUE REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Monetized Servers must:

• submit monthly revenue reports  
• submit zero-revenue reports when applicable  
• disclose all monetization methods  
• disclose all sponsorships  
• disclose all advertising revenue  
• provide transaction logs  
• provide payment processor statements  

Failure to report revenue is treated as **revenue concealment**.

See: Revenue_Reporting_Requirements.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — SERVER MONETIZATION AUDITS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Monetized Servers are subject to:

• scheduled audits  
• random audits  
• triggered audits  
• emergency audits  

Audits may include:

• revenue verification  
• payment platform inspection  
• monetization configuration review  
• log verification  
• sponsorship/advertising review  

Servers must:

• cooperate fully  
• provide documents within 7 days  
• not delete logs  
• not alter monetization systems during audits  

Refusal to cooperate is a **severe violation**.

See: Monetization_Audit_Procedure.md

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — USER PROTECTION REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must:

• clearly disclose prices  
• clearly disclose what users receive  
• avoid deceptive or manipulative design  
• avoid monetization targeting minors  
• provide refunds where required by law  

Servers must NOT:

• use dark patterns  
• hide gameplay behind paywalls  
• misrepresent cosmetic items  
• pressure users into purchases  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — COSMETIC ITEM REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Cosmetic items sold on Servers must:

• be cosmetic-only  
• not affect gameplay  
• not alter stats or hitboxes  
• not obscure visibility  
• not resemble official MultiPixel assets without approval  

Servers must label cosmetic items accurately.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — SPONSORSHIP & ADVERTISING RULES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers may use sponsorships and advertising if:

• they are disclosed  
• they are non-intrusive  
• they do not interrupt gameplay  
• they do not mislead users  
• they do not imply official partnership  
• they do not promote harmful content  

Servers must NOT:

• use forced video ads  
• use mid-game popups  
• use ads targeting minors with monetization  
• use political sponsorships without approval  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 10 — ENFORCEMENT & PENALTIES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Violations of these rules may result in:

• warnings  
• corrective actions  
• temporary suspension  
• permanent suspension  
• delisting  
• monetization bans  
• legal action  
• license termination  

See:
• Server_Violation_Categories.md  
• Server_Penalty_Tiers.md  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 11 — AUTOMATIC SEVERE VIOLATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following ALWAYS result in immediate enforcement:

• pay-to-win monetization  
• selling progression, stats, or power  
• gambling systems  
• crypto/NFT monetization  
• revenue concealment  
• deleted payment logs  
• refusal to cooperate with audits  
• monetization targeting minors  
• unapproved monetization on unapproved Servers  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 12 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document may be updated at any time.  
All Servers must comply with the latest version.
