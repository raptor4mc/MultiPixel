# Server Moderation Standards
Version 1.0 — Relimintor (Raptor4 / MultiPixel)

This document expands Sections 6, 8, 10, 12, and 15 of the MultiPixel
Source-Available License 2.2 — Fortress Edition.

It defines the mandatory moderation requirements for all Servers operating
within the MultiPixel ecosystem.

These standards apply to:
• Public Servers
• Private Servers
• Community Servers
• Business Servers
• Political Servers
• Monetized Servers

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — MODERATION PRINCIPLES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

All Servers must maintain a moderation system that:

• protects users  
• enforces rules consistently  
• prevents harassment and abuse  
• prevents hate speech and extremist content  
• prevents staff abuse  
• maintains fairness and transparency  
• responds to incidents promptly  

Servers must NOT:

• allow unmoderated environments  
• allow staff to act without accountability  
• allow favoritism or discrimination  
• allow retaliation against users who report issues  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 2 — REQUIRED MODERATION SYSTEMS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must implement the following systems:

### 2.1 Moderation Tools
• ban system  
• mute system  
• kick system  
• warning system  
• staff action logging  
• appeal system  

### 2.2 Automated Protections
• chat filters  
• spam detection  
• anti-harassment filters  
• anti-extremism filters  
• anti-grooming protections (if minors are present)  

### 2.3 Reporting Tools
• in-game reporting  
• out-of-game reporting  
• staff escalation channels  

### 2.4 Staff Infrastructure
• clear staff hierarchy  
• defined staff roles  
• staff onboarding and training  
• staff accountability systems  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 3 — MODERATOR RESPONSIBILITIES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Moderators must:

• enforce rules fairly  
• act professionally  
• avoid personal conflicts  
• avoid favoritism  
• avoid harassment  
• document actions  
• escalate severe incidents  
• protect minors  
• maintain confidentiality  

Moderators must NOT:

• abuse power  
• retaliate against users  
• ignore safety incidents  
• engage in harassment  
• use moderation tools for personal disputes  
• leak internal information  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 4 — PROHIBITED MODERATION PRACTICES
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must NOT allow:

### 4.1 Selling Staff Roles
• selling moderator roles  
• selling admin roles  
• selling staff permissions  
• selling ban immunity  

### 4.2 Staff Abuse
• banning users without cause  
• muting users for personal reasons  
• targeting users unfairly  
• using staff powers to win arguments  
• using staff powers to gain gameplay advantages  

### 4.3 Unlogged Actions
• unlogged bans  
• unlogged mutes  
• unlogged kicks  
• unlogged warnings  
• unlogged staff actions  

### 4.4 Untrained Staff
• staff without training  
• staff without knowledge of rules  
• staff without access to guidelines  

Any violation of this section is a **severe violation**.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 5 — REQUIRED MODERATION LOGS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must maintain logs for:

• bans  
• mutes  
• kicks  
• warnings  
• staff actions  
• appeals  
• safety incidents  
• rule enforcement  

Logs must be:

• accurate  
• timestamped  
• unmodified  
• securely stored  
• retained for required periods  

Deleting moderation logs is a **severe violation**.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 6 — USER REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must:

• provide clear reporting tools  
• allow anonymous reporting (if possible)  
• acknowledge reports promptly  
• investigate reports fairly  
• take action within reasonable time  
• protect reporters from retaliation  

Servers must NOT:

• ignore reports  
• dismiss reports without review  
• punish users for reporting issues  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 7 — APPEALS SYSTEM
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must provide an appeals process for:

• bans  
• mutes  
• kicks  
• major moderation actions  

The appeals system must:

• be accessible  
• be documented  
• be reviewed by staff  
• be logged  
• be resolved within reasonable time  

Servers must NOT:

• ignore appeals  
• retaliate against appeals  
• deny appeals without review  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 8 — STAFF ACCOUNTABILITY
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must:

• track staff actions  
• review staff behavior regularly  
• remove abusive staff  
• enforce staff rules consistently  
• maintain a staff code of conduct  

Servers must NOT:

• allow staff to act without oversight  
• allow staff to moderate their own disputes  
• allow staff to bypass logging systems  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 9 — MODERATION VIOLATIONS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Examples of **minor violations**:
• missing documentation  
• outdated staff lists  

Examples of **moderate violations**:
• incomplete moderation logs  
• slow response to reports  
• inconsistent rule enforcement  

Examples of **severe violations**:
• selling staff roles  
• staff abusing power  
• unlogged moderation actions  
• ignoring safety incidents  
• deleting moderation logs  
• retaliation against reporters  
• allowing hate speech or harassment  

See:
• Server_Violation_Categories.md  
• Server_Penalty_Tiers.md  

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 10 — AUDIT REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Servers must provide:

• moderation logs  
• staff lists  
• staff training documentation  
• moderation system configuration  
• appeals records  
• safety incident logs  

Refusal to provide moderation data during an audit is a **severe violation**.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 11 — AMENDMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

This document may be updated at any time.  
All Servers must comply with the latest version.
