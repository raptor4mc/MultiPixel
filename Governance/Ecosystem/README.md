MultiPixel Compliance Documentation
This directory contains all compliance‑related documents for the MultiPixel ecosystem. Compliance defines the mandatory rules, enforcement systems, audit procedures, and legal obligations that servers, mods, businesses, and creators must follow to remain authorized within the MultiPixel platform.

Compliance is organized into subfolders based on enforcement domain. Each subfolder contains documents that define requirements, restrictions, and penalties for that specific area.

 Compliance Philosophy
The MultiPixel compliance system is designed to:

protect the integrity of the ecosystem

ensure safe and fair gameplay

enforce monetization and revenue‑share rules

maintain technical and security standards

prevent misuse of branding and identity

ensure proper handling of user data

provide a clear and consistent enforcement framework

Compliance documents are mandatory and apply to all users, servers, mods, and businesses operating within the MultiPixel ecosystem.

Updating Compliance Documents
When updating or adding new compliance documents:

Use the MultiPixel naming convention.

Place the document in the correct subfolder based on its enforcement domain.

Increment the version number inside the document.

Update the filename to match the new version.

Add a note to the Governance CHANGELOG (if present).

 Enforcement & Escalation
Compliance violations may result in:

warnings

temporary or permanent bans

server suspension

mod removal

monetization revocation

financial penalties

legal action

Severe violations may be escalated directly to MultiPixel administration.
